About LIFT

Imagine Your Church Renewed!

  • Imagine tired, worn-out church leaders feeling refreshed again.
  • Imagine discouraged pastors renewing their call.
  • Imagine the people of Christ saying their “Yes” to God all over again.
  • Imagine strangers to God coming home.
  • Imagine 3 days of vibrant worship, biblically sound preaching, heartfelt prayer, repentance, teaching, fellowship, and healing.
  • Imagine your congregation bathing itself in the abundant grace of God!

A 3-Day Renewal experience is within your congregations’ reach. LIFT Ministries is serving the body of Christ by bringing 3-day renewal experiences to local churches. Dynamic preaching highlights each evenings’ worship. But the star of the show is Jesus Christ and the sole purpose is glorifying God and blessing and edifying God’s people. Contact LIFT today to schedule your renewal experience.

What Can We Expect from our Renewal Experience?

  • A 3-month partnership that is covered in prayer
  • Thorough, disciplined preparation
  • Church leaders who are equipped and trained for servant ministry
  • Passionate preaching
  • An expectation that God will move in powerful ways
  • Fresh opportunities to respond to God’s call
  • Church-wide sense of fresh momentum
  • Tools for effective follow-through

What Does LIFT Expect of Our Church?

  • A lead time of at least 90 days
  • The willingness to set down your work during the 3 days of the Renewal Experience
  • A commitment to have the whole congregation participate for the full 3 days
  • The local congregation is asked to cover the travel and hospitality expenses for LIFT (gasoline / lodging, if travelling a distance)
  • A Love Offering will be taken up for LIFT on Sunday morning.
  • If outside musicians are needed, the host church will be asked to provide for their travel and hospitality expenses.

The LIFT Team looks forward to meeting with your Pastor and church leadership to discuss the possibility of bringing a LIFT Renewal Experience to your church. Let us know how we may assist you.

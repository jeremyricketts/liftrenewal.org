About LIFT

“LIFT Presents…”

Once a year, LIFT serves up a multi-day Conference in Southern California for clergy and laity to strengthen their collective work in ministry. The themes will vary from year to year with topics of interest to those who serve the Lord in local churches and beyond. Of course, worship is a vital part of our gathered time and keeps us focused on Christ as we learn and work.

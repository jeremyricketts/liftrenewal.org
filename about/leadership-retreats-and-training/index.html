About LIFT

Big Church, Little Church

Makes no difference the size of your church - LIFT loves to work with local congregations and groups within the Church to help them grow in their discipleship to Jesus Christ. LIFT has retreat leaders available to serve you in a wide variety of areas.

Retreats, Workshops, and Training

Size is not the issue here, either – what counts is your church’s desire to get stronger in ministry and discipleship together. Here’s a brief listing of the areas of ministry focus with which LIFT’s retreat leaders and trainers can assist:

  • Church Council leadership training
  • Worship Design
  • Prayer Ministries
  • Small Group Ministries
  • Ministry Staff Team Building
  • Human Resource Management
  • Men’s and Women’s groups

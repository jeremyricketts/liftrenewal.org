Building a Place for Renewal

LIFT Ministries, in partnership with LIFT in Ghana, is supporting the creation of the LIFT Renewal Center!

Imagine…

  • A place for Spiritual Retreat
  • A place for Leadership Training
  • A place for Visiting Missionaries
  • A place to host the Walk to Emmaus

In the U.S. we are blessed with the availability of comfortable, affordable facilities. But the Methodist Church in Ghana has very limited facilities for pastors and laypersons to take retreat for spiritual renewal,apart from expensive hotels.

Together we can make this happen - We are blessed to be a blessing!

LIFT Ministries is raising money in order to build this facility which will be used for all these purposes as well as hosting Walk to Emmaus weekends.

Thus far, LIFT has raised $5,000 to build the Center ! The remaining cost to complete the construction is approximately $60,000. You can support this vital project by making a tax-deductible gift to LIFT Ministries in any amount.

Visit www.LiftRenewal.org and click “Donate”.

Thank-You!

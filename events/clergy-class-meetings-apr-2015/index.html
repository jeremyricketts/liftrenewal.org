Clergy Class Meetings

May 06, 2015

Trinity United Methodist Church, San Bernardino
224 E. 16th Street, San Bernardino, CA 9244 map

Every Wednesday morning at 10:30am, Pastors from churches in the Inland Empire gather for a traditional Wesleyan Class Meeting. May Class Meetings are at St. Trinity United Methodist Church in San Bernardino. All clergy are welcome. Location changes monthly. Contact LIFT for more information.

Free Seminar: The General Rule of Discipleship

May 05, 2018

First United Methodist Church of Orange
161 S. Orange Street, Orange, CA 92866 map

The General Rule of Discipleship: A Rule of Life for Disciple-Making Congregations

Presented by John Wesley Scholar and National Church Leader Rev. Steve Manskar

In this workshop, we will: • Explore the habits Jesus practiced and taught his disciples • Learn why it takes both spirit and discipline to make a Christian • Develop a “congregational rule of life” based on love of God and love of neighbor • Learn effective ways to make disciples of Jesus Christ

9am to 3pm Laypersons and Clergy encouraged to attend

Lunch is available for $10, or bring a bag lunch. Please RSVP for lunch reservations by Thursday, May 4. (714) 532-6363

Co-Sponsored by First United Methodist Church of Orange, and LIFT Ministries

Ghana Mission Team Meeting

March 07, 2015

By God’s grace, LIFT will again organize a Team to serve in Ghana this summer. If you would like to learn about this mission and be considered for the Team, you’re invited to attend an information meeting at the LIFT Ministries office on Saturday, March 7th at 10am.. If you have any questions ahead of time, please call Judy Gillham, Ministry Coordinator, at (562) 789-0880, or click the “Contact” tab to email LIFT.

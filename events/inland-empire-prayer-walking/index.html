Inland Empire Prayer Walking

January 10, 2015

St. Paul's United Methodist Church
785 N. Arrowhead Ave., San Bernardino, CA 92401 map

Saturday - February 14 - is the next LIFT Prayer Walk in the St. Paul’s United Methodist Church in San Bernardino. On the 2nd Saturday of each month we start out from a different church in the Inland Empire area in order to cover our communities with Prayer and Listen to God.

All are welcome! Meet at St. Paul’s UMC at 9am. We’ll have a brief time of worship and prayer together, then form small groups to walk and pray silently through the community. A couple people will be asked to remain in the Sanctuary to keep vigil while we walk, interceding for the walkers. After about 45 minutes or so, we’ll return to the church to share what God was showing us, then pray, and return home. Come and be a blessing in the community!

And this is the confidence that we have toward him, that if we ask anything according to his will he hears us. 1 John 5:14

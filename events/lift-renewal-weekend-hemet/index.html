LIFT Renewal Weekend

January 21, 2016 — Jan 23, 2016

Hemet United Methodist Church
530. S. Buena Vista St., Hemet, CA 92543 map

In January, Hemet UMC hosts a 3-Day Renewal weekend that will feature meaningful worship and prayer,in-depth Bible study, retreat-time for church leaders and staff, and warm Christian fellowship, all focusing on drawing closer to the heart of God and deepening their discipleship to Jesus Christ. Worship every evening at 7pm The preacher for the Weekend is the Rev. Bill Johnson.

LIFT Renewal Weekend

January 25, 2018 — January 27, 2018

Shepherd of the Valley United Methodist Church
45501 Deglet Noor St, Indio, CA 92201 map

In January,2018, Shepherd of the Valley UMC hosts a 3-Day Renewal weekend that will feature meaningful worship and prayer, in-depth Bible study, retreat-time for church leaders and staff, and warm Christian fellowship, all focusing on drawing closer to the heart of God and deepening their discipleship to Jesus Christ. Worship every evening at 7pm The preacher for the Weekend is the Rev. Bill Johnson.

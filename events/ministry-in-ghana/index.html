Ministry in Ghana

August 11, 2015

Accra, Ghana

A Team of 6 clergy and laypersons will travel to Accra, Ghana, in West Africa, this summer for a third mission trip to strengthen local church leaders in disciple-making. Your prayers are welcomed as they travel and serve!

The past 2 years have focused on the “Big 5” of discipleship and building a Discipling Network which reaches out beyond the walls of the church. Following up on those previous seminars, LIFT this year will have conversation with a core group of leaders to encourage them forward in their efforts.

LIFT Team members will also take part in evening Revival Worship services and preach in local congregations on Sunday morning. A visit to the “LIFT Renewal Center” currently under construction will be included, to view the progress of this special project and pray with the construction team.

The LIFT Mission Team members:

  • Rev. William Johnson
  • Dr. Bob Denham
  • Rev. Tonya Harris
  • Rev. Tom Rothhaar
  • David Nesamony
  • Judy Gillham

Mission dates are: August 11 through 22, 2015.

Your support for this work is greatly appreciated! Prayer sustains the Team, and financial support helps with the cost of supplies. There is also an opportunity to support the building of the LIFT Renewal Center.

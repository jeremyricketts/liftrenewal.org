Prayer Walking

November 14, 2015 — Nov 14, 2015

Emmanuel United Methodist Church
3310 North G St. San Bernardino, CA. 92405 map

Cover the Inland Empire with Prayer

Every 2nd Saturday of the month, LIFT Ministries goes Prayer Walking in the community surrounding the church. There is time for worship and prayer together, then, in small groups, we walk through the neighborhood to listen to God and lift up the community in prayer. Come be a blessing. Come bring light and peace into the world! See you at EMMANUEL UMC in San Bernardino! Gather at 9am in the Sanctuary.

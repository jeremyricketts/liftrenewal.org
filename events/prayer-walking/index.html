Prayer Walking-February 14

February 14, 2015

Cover the Inland Empire with Prayer - February 14th, 9 a.m. at St. Paul’s United Methodist Church in San Bernardino.

Every 2nd Saturday of the month, LIFT Ministries goes Prayer Walking in the community surrounding the church. There is time for worship and prayer together, then in small groups, we walk silently through the neighborhood to listen to God and lift up the community in prayer. Come be a blessing. Come bring light and peace into the world! See you at St. Paul’s UMC in San Bernardino!

Spoke 'N Word

February 25, 2017

Worship, Cycle, Eat - Repeat! Join up with a new cycling group led by Rev. Bill Johnson. The fellowship rides will rotate through the Cal-Pac Districts on the Mainland, with the next event scheduled for Saturday, February 25, location to be announced soon! Gather at 8am for a brief time of worship, then enjoy a 30-40-mile ride, followed by lunch prepared by LIFT Ministries.

Spoke N Word is open to all persons able to cycle moderate to long distances. Helmets and water bottles are a MUST. Cost for lunch is $8.00.
